Privacy Policy for InstaMake

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

InstaMake ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

We are committed to a "Privacy-First" approach: we do not store your photos or videos on any servers.

1. Data Collection & Usage

User Content (Photos & Videos): All editing and collage creation happens locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your media on our servers.

Third-Party Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to understand how users interact with our app.

Firebase may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFV)
  • General location data (e.g., city/country)
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • Device model and OS version

Purpose: This data is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

2. Data Storage & Security

Since your media remains on your device, we cannot see or share it.

For the data collected via Firebase, Google employs industry-standard security measures. You can review Google’s privacy practices here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
